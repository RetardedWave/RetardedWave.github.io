Stupid is dead.
Stupid was a bot i made back in 2017, under the name "WaveBot". Then it was just a basic bot, as i was just learning JavaScript. All through 2018, it gained commands and got to its peak of 88 servers. It had a LOT of downtime in the beggining as i didnt have a host yet. Towards mid 2018 the bot was moved to github and was getting hosted on Heroku. Heroku wasn't ideal for it but it worked. I got it up to 20 commands before attempting a database, where everything turned south. The database would just reset every time the bot restarted, which heroku automatically does atleast once every 24 hours. Me being the lazy person i am, decided to not find a fix. So in February of 2019, I deleted all the code and killed the bot for good.
